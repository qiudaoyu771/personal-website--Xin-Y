At the same time, I also like to read books in the coffee shop, enjoying a peaceful and cozy. Besides coffee shops, I also love reading. I like to pick up all kinds of books, from literature to business and economy, from science and technology to history and humanities, each book is a time for me to explore and think about the world. In general, I like to enrich and enrich my life in various ways, whether it's savoring the coffee culture in a coffee shop, drawing knowledge and inspiration from books, or unleashing my creativity through photography and LEGO, all of these have become a part of my life, bringing me endless fun and satisfaction.
